Maa’r Story

Maa is a Bangladeshi mother who loves cooking at the comfort of her own home.

She moved from Bangladesh to USA right after marriage and learned to cook so many dishes on her own. What started off as just experiments and hoping that the food is edible became a passion. She received compliments from so many people and everyone suggested to start catering.

Life went on and she never had the chance to actually give that suggestion a serious thought. After about 30 years or so, finally, time has come and now she has decided to share her hobby with the world.
